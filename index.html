Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada, is a versatile artist known for his chart-topping music and entrepreneurial ventures. Rising from his acting roots in "Degrassi: The Next Generation," Drake quickly made a mark in the music industry with hits like "Hotline Bling" and "God's Plan." Beyond music, he's founded the record label OVO Sound and the clothing line OVO (October's Very Own), showcasing his influence both on and off the stage.

Kanye West, born on June 8, 1977, in Atlanta, Georgia, is an influential force in music, fashion, and culture. Emerging as a producer for Roc-A-Fella Records, West's solo career soared with groundbreaking albums like "The College Dropout" and "Late Registration." His impact transcends music, with ventures like the Yeezy clothing line and collaborations with top designers reshaping the fashion landscape. Despite controversy, West's artistic vision and boundary-pushing creativity cement his status as a cultural icon.
