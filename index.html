Drake

Drake is signed to record labels including OVO Sound, Warner Bros. Records, and Republic Records. Fun facts about him include his nickname "Drizzy" and his acting role as Jimmy Brooks on the TV show Degrassi: The Next Generation.

Biography

Aubrey Drake Graham, known professionally as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and businessman. Born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Ontario, Drake gained recognition as an actor on the teen drama television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" in the early 2000s.

Kanye West

Kanye West, also known as Ye, is associated with record labels like Roc-A-Fella Records, Def Jam Recordings, and GOOD Music. Fun facts about him include his ventures into fashion with his Yeezy brand and his outspoken personality, often sparking controversy in the media.

Biography

Kanye Omari West is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and fashion designer. Born on June 8, 1977, in Atlanta, Georgia, West first became known as a producer for Roc-A-Fella Records. He gained widespread fame with his 2004 album "The College Dropout" and has since released several critically acclaimed albums, including "Late Registration" (2005), "Graduation" (2007), and "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy" (2010).
